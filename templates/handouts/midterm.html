Logistics

The CS106A Midterm is Monday Feb 13th from 7pm to 9pm. We will provide more details (what the exam covers, practice exams) closer to the date.

If you have OAE accommodations, or if you have a conflict with this time, and absolutely cannot make the regularly scheduled midterm, you must send a request by email to me (piech@cs.stanford.edu) by 5:00pm on Sunday, Feb 5th to arrange an alternate exam time.
